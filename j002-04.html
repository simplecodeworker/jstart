Scanner Tokens

The Scanner uses whitespace (spaces, tabs, newlines) as default delimiters. nextInt() only reads the digits—the newline character remains in the buffer!

Format Precision

In printf, precision rounds numbers to the nearest value. %.2f rounds 1.555 to 1.56, not 1.55.
